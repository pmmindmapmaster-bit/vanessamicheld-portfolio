About

The work is about people, not just projects.

I am a Senior Implementation Leader with 16+ years of experience driving enterprise technology programs, system integrations, and organizational transformation initiatives across New York City and beyond.

My philosophy is direct: tools and systems do not deliver outcomes. People do. My role is to inspire, protect, and align the teams who build the solutions. I specialize in complex, multi-stakeholder environments where communication, trust, and clarity determine success.

I integrate AI tools and automation into how I plan, communicate, and deliver — helping teams move faster without losing the human connection that makes projects actually work.

I am also a Reiki Master and meditation practitioner. That grounding shapes how I lead. I bring calm focus, intentional listening, and servant leadership to every engagement, no matter the pressure.

Servant Leadership

I serve the team so the team can serve the mission. Removing blockers, building trust, and protecting my people is my core function.

Energy Leadership

How people show up determines what gets delivered. I manage team energy, morale, and engagement as a strategic project asset.

Mindful Execution

Grounded in meditation and Reiki practice, I bring intentional presence, resilience, and clarity to high-pressure environments.

Strategic Delivery

From initiation through closeout, I align portfolio execution with business strategy, risk management, and measurable outcomes.
